Our Services

Our foundation offers different services to enable us curb the environmental degradation and support those affected by it.

Desertification

Through out the years desrtification has become a diverse problem in our country thus the kenya green foundation has been building dams in arid and semi-arid areas which assists in water cathment and the water is used by the people for domestic use and irrigation. also the foundation assists in tree planting programmes that involves the communities.

plastic waste

the plastic wastes are collected from our own dampsites and others are being sold to our company which later on they are taken to our factories for recycling.The plastics are made into useful things which are sold and also expoted.

Employment

people who are creative are given a chance to show their creativity through coming up with different designs from the recycled plastics. Also some people are givenjobs in the factory and some are paid when they bring the waste materials in our company.By this we ensure no young person stays idle and engages in vices and that people are able to put food on their table.

Export

Our products from the recycled plastics are sold to other countries as piece of art intern increasing sales and also educating other people on environmental conversation.

Water

Our foundation has opened water points in the arid and semi-arid areas which is clean for the communities to use hence reducing diseases caused by drinking dirty water.This is not only done in the desert areas but also in the slums also our company supplies chloroquin to the people to ensure the water is safe.
