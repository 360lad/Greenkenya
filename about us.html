The organisation started as a small community which was headed by young person known as Abdul Latif who thought of making the environment conducive to all organisms. the organisation was able to start through small funds from well wishers and later on the government and other organisations joined through partnerships.

the organisation has been able to achieve much of its objective it has been able to build dams that assist in storage of water in both the arid and also semi-arid areas.it has also asisted in reducing the environmental pollution by educating members of the community on ways of waste disposal and providing the necessary tools to implement that.

the organisation is planning on making more partnerships at international level and opening more branches across East Africa.

Our offices are located at Kinyanjui streets opposite Kinyanjui high school in Thika town
